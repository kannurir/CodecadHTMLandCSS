SUKHIBHAVA

SUKHIBHAVA is an app that can ease your life by organizing your health information. In SUKHIBHAVA we store information of your loved one and also ask your personal informaton so that we can have keep track and analyze it for best recommenadations and atricles.
With SUKHIBHAVA you can:

  • Save Medical Information and test results on your personal electronic portal very conveniently
  • Share the data with your beloved ones anywhere across the globe more securely
  • Never miss a pill with our timely reminders

SUKHIBHAVA can be accesed through:

  1. Mobile Application IOS Andriod
  2. Web Application

In SUKHIBHAVA, you will be adding your picture, address details, DOB, emergency contact details, Vitals, security questions, payment information.
